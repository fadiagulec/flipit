# AI Video Prompts — "Teaching Claude" B-roll set

Four shot prompts for Sora 2 / Veo 3.1 / Kling 3 Omni. Hero shot is the must-do; the other three are upgrades.

**Avatar lock (do not change across renders):**
- Mediterranean features, late 20s
- Long very dark brown hair (style varies per shot — see each prompt)
- Fair-medium olive complexion, faint freckles
- Warm brown eyes, defined dark brows
- Single small gold hoop earring
- Slim build

**Vary across shots:** outfit, hairstyle, makeup intensity, setting only.

---

## SHOT A — HERO SHOT · "Teaching Claude" (8s)

The single most important render. Pairs with the entire 70-second founder-story script.

```
8-second cinematic vertical 9:16 close-up to medium shot, single continuous take, golden-hour interior.

SUBJECT: A woman in her late twenties with Mediterranean features — long dark brown hair pulled into a sleek high ponytail with face-framing strands escaping at the temples, fair-medium olive complexion, faint freckles across the nose, warm brown eyes, defined dark brows, prominent cheekbones, a single small gold hoop earring. She is wearing a fitted ribbed cream knit sweater with the sleeves pushed up to her elbows, and rests one hand on a laptop keyboard. Her exact face geometry, skin tone, hair color, and eye color must remain identical across every frame — do not morph her.

SETTING: A warm minimalist home studio. Late afternoon sun coming through a window from camera-left, soft amber 2700K. Behind her, a clean wall layered with handwritten sticky notes in pastel pinks and yellows — ideas, prompt fragments, single words like "hook," "loop," "ship." On the desk: an open MacBook angled slightly toward camera, a stack of three matte hardcover books with cracked spines (titles legible: "DESIGN OF EVERYDAY THINGS", "THE PRAGMATIC PROGRAMMER", "MAKE BOOK"), a paper notebook with a fountain pen resting on it, a half-drunk matcha in a small ceramic cup, and a folded pair of round glasses. A small green plant in the corner.

ON THE LAPTOP SCREEN, clearly visible from the angle: the Claude.ai chat interface — clean white background, the recognizable orange-to-purple gradient circular Claude logo top-left, a conversation thread visible with a user message in a rounded grey bubble at the top and a longer Claude response below in a white bubble with a faint left border. Some lines of code visible inside the response, syntax-highlighted in muted teal and pink. The cursor is blinking inside a new prompt input at the bottom.

ACTION ARC, no cuts:

[0–2s] She is reading the screen, brow furrowed slightly in genuine concentration — not exaggerated. Her right hand types two unhurried keystrokes. The screen flickers as a new Claude response begins streaming line by line. The light from the screen catches her eyes faintly.

[2–4.5s] She pauses, leans back an inch, lifts her gaze from the screen and looks slightly off-camera-right as if processing what just appeared. A small genuine half-smile begins at the right corner of her mouth — the smile of someone who just got something to work. She picks up the pen, writes one quick word in her paper notebook without breaking her thoughtful expression.

[4.5–7s] She turns her head slowly toward the camera. Her eyes meet the lens directly, warm, confident, knowing. The half-smile deepens — full Duchenne smile, eye-corners crinkling, shoulders rolling back slightly. The amber sunlight crosses her face.

[7–8s] Hold on her face. She nods once, almost imperceptibly. Final beat: a single sticky note in soft focus behind her right shoulder reads in handwritten script: "you do not need permission."

CAMERA: Locked-off mid-shot, framing her from mid-chest up with the laptop screen and one stack of books visible in frame. Subtle natural breathing-level handheld micro-movement only. No zoom, no pan.

LENS / LOOK: 50mm equivalent, f/2.0 shallow depth of field, soft natural skin texture preserved (do NOT smooth out pores or freckles), Kodak Portra 400 color science — warm highlights in golden-amber, gentle teal in the shadows. Cinematic, intimate, true-to-life. The Claude interface on the laptop screen should remain crisp and legible throughout, not blurred out.

AUDIO: Soft ambient room tone, the faint ticking of a wall clock, distant city noise outside the window, a single quiet keyboard click around 1.5 seconds. No music. No dialogue.

NEGATIVE: no captions, no on-screen title text, no logos other than the Claude logo on the screen, no morphing of her face between frames, no cartoonish exaggerated expressions, no smile before second 3, no cuts, no zoom, no extra people in frame, no AI plastic skin texture, no makeup change, no wardrobe change, no hair-color change.
```

## SHOT C — "Sticky Note Wall" (5s)

Profile shot of her placing a new sticky note. Pairs with "the future is being built by people who decided."

```
5-second cinematic vertical 9:16 medium shot. The same woman from the hero shot — locked features, sleek high ponytail with face-framing strands, ribbed cream knit sweater, gold hoop earring, faint freckles, olive skin. Standing in profile against a wall layered with dozens of pastel sticky notes covered in handwritten phrases: "hook first", "stop starting from zero", "ship ugly", "ninety days". She is holding a fresh pink sticky note in her right hand. She slowly lifts her hand and presses it carefully onto an empty space on the wall. The new note reads in clean handwritten script: "you don't need permission". She holds her hand on it for one beat, lets go, takes one small step back, and tilts her head looking at the wall with a small private smile. Warm 2700K window light from camera-left. 50mm f/2.0, Kodak Portra color, freckles preserved. No on-screen text, no captions, no morphing, no other people, no music, no cartoonish style.
```

No captions, no music, no morphing, no other people, no AI plastic skin.
